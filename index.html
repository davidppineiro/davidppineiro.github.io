David Pérez Piñeiro

email / github / scholar / linkedin

I am a research scientist at Gridmatic, where I build optimization models and algorithms for quantitative energy trading.

I am interested in convex optimization and its applications to control, energy, and finance. I am a PhD candidate at NTNU advised by Sigurd Skogestad. I received my BS in Energy Engineering from the University of Vigo and my MS in Sustainable Energy from NTNU, supported by a "la Caixa" Foundation fellowship, and did research stays with Richard Braatz at MIT and Stephen Boyd at Stanford.
